Welcome to Our Data Visualization Guide

This guide will help you understand how to interact with the visualization of our extensive drug database.
For the best user experience, use your laptop as the database might be heavy for a mobile phone.

Step 1: Zooming In and Out

Use the scroll wheel on your mouse or the pinch gesture on a touchpad to zoom in and out of the visualization. This will help you get a closer look at the data or see the overall picture.

Step 2: Exploring Drug Connections

Hover your mouse over the orange circles to see the name of each drug. Lines connecting the circles represent drugs that are grouped together.

Step 3: Navigate and have Fun!

Press-hold on one of the dots and drag the groups around.
Press-hold on the background to navigate without zooming in and out.

Understanding the Scale

The visualization contains a comprehensive view of thousands of drugs in our database. The purpose is to showcase the vastness of the data we have collected.
